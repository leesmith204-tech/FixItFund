Renovation Loan Programs

7 Ways to Finance a Fixer-Upper

Every program below wraps your purchase price and renovation costs into one single mortgage — no second loan, no paying out of pocket for repairs before moving in.

FHA · Limited

FHA 203(k) Limited

The streamlined renovation loan for cosmetic and non-structural work. Covers kitchens, flooring, roofing, HVAC, windows, paint, and more — anything that doesn't require structural engineering or foundation work. Recently updated to allow up to $75,000 in renovation costs. Faster and simpler to close than the Standard 203k.

3.5% min down 580+ credit Up to $75K reno Primary residence No structural work
Apply with a Renovation Loan Advisor →
FHA · Standard

FHA 203(k) Standard

The full-power renovation loan for major repairs and structural work. Covers additions, foundation repairs, septic systems, full gut renovations — anything goes. Required when renovation costs exceed $75,000 or when structural work is needed. Requires a HUD-approved 203k consultant to manage the draw schedule and inspections. Best for serious fixer-uppers.

3.5% min down 580+ credit $75K+ reno Primary residence Structural work OK
Apply with a Renovation Loan Advisor →
Conventional

HomeStyle Renovation

A conventional renovation loan with no FHA mortgage insurance fees. Great for buyers with stronger credit putting 10-20% down. Covers any renovation type including luxury upgrades and landscaping. Investment properties and second homes are allowed — making it ideal for house hackers and investors.

3% min down 620+ credit No FHA fees Investment props OK Any reno type
Apply with a Renovation Loan Advisor →
Conventional

CHOICERenovation

Very similar to HomeStyle Renovation — same 3% down, same conventional loan structure, same investment property eligibility. The key difference is CHOICERenovation includes a resilience improvement credit for upgrades that protect against storms, floods, wildfires, and other natural hazards. A great alternative to HomeStyle, especially for properties in coastal, flood zone, or weather-prone markets. Your loan advisor can compare both and recommend the better fit.

3% min down 620+ credit Resilience credit Investment props OK No FHA fees Any reno type

Conventional · Fast

RenoExpress

The fastest renovation loan on the market. Designed for lighter cosmetic updates under $75,000 with a streamlined approval process and no HUD consultant required. Perfect for cosmetic updaters and undervalued homes that just need modernizing. Closes faster than any other renovation loan.

3% min down 620+ credit Up to $75K reno Fastest close No consultant needed

VA · Veterans

VA Renovation Loan

The most powerful renovation loan available — and it's exclusively for veterans, active duty service members, and surviving spouses. Zero down payment, no private mortgage insurance, and competitive rates. Combines purchase and renovation into one loan with no monthly MI. One of the best financial tools available for eligible buyers.

$0 down No PMI ever 620+ credit Veterans only Primary residence

USDA · Rural

USDA Renovation Loan

Zero down payment for eligible rural and suburban properties. Many properties just outside major metros qualify — not just farms and countryside. Wraps purchase and renovation into one low-cost loan with below-market rates. Income limits apply. Use the USDA eligibility map to check a specific address. Ideal for undervalued and cosmetic-update homes in suburban markets.

$0 down Low fees 640+ credit USDA zones only Income limits apply
